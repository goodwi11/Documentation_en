=================
AI Page Generator
=================

Overview
========

| **AI Page Generator** is a universal ChatGPT-based tool for generating safe pages using a text prompt. Unlike other generators, where a selected theme or an app link is used to create a safe page, AI Page allows you to describe the page you need in your own words and get a ready-made safe page based on your request.

| The generator is suitable for creating pages for different niches, approaches, and advertising scenarios when a more flexible setup of the structure, meaning, and content presentation is required. Thanks to the text prompt, you can define the general direction of the page, the desired content format, and the key focus points of the future safe page.

How to generate a safe page
---------------------------

.. note::

   The selected generator deducts your available limit specifically for generating the safe page preview, not for downloading the archive.

**Step 1.** Select the **AI Page** generator on the generation page and enter a text description of the page you want to generate in the "Prompt" field.

Example prompt::

   A website about financial literacy, personal budgeting, expense control methods, and money-saving tips

| **Step 2.** Enter keywords and the domain name in the corresponding fields.
| Example keywords (enter separated by commas): `key, keyword1, keyword2`
| Example domain name: `https://example.com` or `example.com`

| **Step 3.** Click the **"Generate"** button. After a short time, you will be shown a preview of the page.
| `If desired, you can generate the safe page again to get a different version of the design and content.`

| **Step 4.** Click the **"Download"** button to get a ZIP archive with the ready-made safe page.

| **Step 5.** Unpack the downloaded ZIP archive and upload it to your server or hosting.
