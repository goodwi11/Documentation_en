=====
Tools
=====

.. note::
Tools are available to all users with an active **BASIC**, **PRO** or **BUSINESS** subscription.

This section contains a set of utilities designed to simplify workflows when creating and preparing content for affiliate marketing tasks. 
Each tool serves its own purpose — from generating data to uniquizing images and videos.

Uniqueizers
===========

Uniqueizers are designed to modify images and videos to increase their uniqueness.
This tool is useful for arbitrage campaigns, content testing, and avoiding repeated media detection.

Photo Uniqueizer
----------------
Allows you to upload an image and transform it into a unique version while preserving visual quality.
Three levels of uniquization are available: Light, Medium, Strong.

**How to Use**

**Step 1.** Upload an image (formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP and others).

**Step 2.** Select the desired degree of uniquization.

**Step 3.** Click **“Make it unique”**.

**Step 4.** After processing, a preview of the resulting image will appear.

**Step 5.** Click **“Download”** to save the final version.

Video Uniqueizer
----------------

Allows you to upload a video and transform it into a unique version while preserving visual quality.

**How to Use**

**Step 1.** Upload a video file (formats: MP4, WEBP and others).

**Step 2.** Select the desired degree of uniquization.

**Step 3.** Click **“Make it unique”**.

**Step 4.** After processing, a preview of the resulting video will appear.

**Step 5.** Click **“Download”** to save the final version.

Data Generators
===============

Data generators are designed to create text-based data, such as addresses and phone numbers, according to the standards of the selected country.
These tools help quickly obtain valid data for filling forms, landing pages and advertising materials.

Address Generator
-----------------

Allows you to generate a random address that follows the format of the selected country.

**How to Use**

**Step 1.** Select a country from the dropdown list.

**Step 2.** Click “Generate”.

**Step 3.** The result will appear in the “Generated address” field.

**Step 4.** Click “Copy” to save the address to your clipboard.

Phone Number Generator
----------------------

Allows you to generate a random phone number that matches the dialing code and structure of the selected country.

**How to Use**

**Step 1.** Select a country from the dropdown list.

**Step 2.** Click **“Generate”**.

**Step 3.** The result will appear in the **“Generated number”** field.

**Step 4.** Click **“Copy”** to save the number to your clipboard.
